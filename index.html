Terms & Conditions

The Terms & Conditions for this Last Man Standing Competition are as follows:

  • Selection Process: Each participant must pick one team to win in each weekly round of fixtures. If the selected team wins, the participant progresses to the next round. Lose/Draw and the participant is eliminated from the remainder of the competition.
  • Unique Selections: Once a team has been selected it cannot be chosen again for the duration of the competition. This means participants must strategise their picks carefully as the competition progresses. If a team is selected a second time an email will be returned, notifying you, and you will be prompted to pick another team.
  • Deadline for picks: Participants must make their weekly selections 1hour before kick-off of the first fixture of that gameweek. If a selection is not made in time, the system will assign a random team from that participant's remaining team picks.
  • Matches: If a match that is due to be played is postponed or cancelled, the participant will automatically advance, but that team will no longer be available for future selections.
  • End of Competition: The competition continues until only one participant remains with a winning record. If all participants are eliminated in a round, that round ends without a winner, and a new round may be created.
  • Agreeing to T's & C's: By registering into this Last Man Standing Competition, you are agreeing to the Terms and Conditions as they are presented.
