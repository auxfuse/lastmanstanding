Yvonne's Story

In August 2024, just six weeks after giving birth to our wonderful daughter Fiadh, my wife Yvonne was diagnosed with Stage 4 colorectal cancer. We started her treatment journey in Ireland after she was given a poor prognosis and told to try chemotherapy. Unfortunately, this is just an attempt to prolong her life without any hope of remission, and it hasn't really worked out so far, with her condition worsening.
